Reviews

I loved it, bucket list checked

My friends and I decided to go for the hot air balloon ride on our trip to GC. Though I have fear of the heights and was having mixed feelings prior to it, I never regretted the decision to go for it. The experience up there was amazing and I even got to witness a real life proposal up in the air! Many thanks to Ben, our pilot, for the great flight and for ensuring that our landing is a safe one.

The best thing we have done together

I took my girlfriend for a surprise hot air balloon trip and we have absolutely enjoyed our time up in the air. We live in two different states and to see the world up in the air together was just magical. It was breath- taking, fresh and peaceful. The hot air team are very friendly and helpful and make the journey quiet the experience. I recommend it to anyone.
